## Database Overview
The EchoNet-Dynamic database is a public dataset published by Stanford University, which includes 10,030 labeled echocardiogram videos and human expert annotations (measurements, tracings, and calculations) to provide a baseline to study cardiac motion and chamber sizes.
You can find the database at https://echonet.github.io/dynamic/
